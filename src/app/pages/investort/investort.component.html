Learn the basics!


Bullish +

Positive trend for the share on the stock market

FOMO

Fear Of Missing Out

Diamond hands

Having and strong position and holding it for the long term

Bearish -

Negative trend for the share on the stock market


REITs

Real Estate Investment Trusts


Paper hands

Having and insecure position and panic selling


Ask

The lowest price a seller is willing to accept when selling a security (stock).


Dollar Cost Average

Adding money to the shares over time regardless the price


Blue chip stock

A stock with a good record of keeping up with the market,usually low risk low reward


Bid

The highest price a buyer is willing to accept when purchasing a security (stock).


Spread

The difference between Ask and Bid.


Crash

A large sell-off 10% or more in the stock market in a single day
